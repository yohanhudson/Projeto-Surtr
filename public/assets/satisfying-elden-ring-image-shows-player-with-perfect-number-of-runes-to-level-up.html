Satisfying Elden Ring Image Shows Player With Perfect Number of Runes to Level Up

2 months ago MrHelstein

Much like other FromSofteware titles, Elden Ring has players using points (runes in this case) to level up their characters. Runes can be acquired from killing enemies and distributed into the different stats that affect things like a player’s health pool, weapon damage, etc. As one player has shown, sometimes it’s possible to have just the perfect number of runes when it comes to leveling up in Elden Ring.
